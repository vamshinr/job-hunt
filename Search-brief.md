## Strong Match Signals

A role is a strong match if it includes 3 or more of:

* Building or optimizing LLM inference systems
* Model serving at scale
* AI infrastructure or ML platform engineering
* RAG, retrieval, vector search, or knowledge graph systems
* Agent runtime, agent memory, tool-use, or orchestration
* Distributed systems or production ML infrastructure
* GPU, CUDA, Triton, quantization, or low-level performance work
* Benchmarking, profiling, latency, throughput, or memory optimization
* Python plus systems programming
* Enterprise AI platform or developer tooling

## Dealbreakers — Skip These

Do not include roles that:

* Are unpaid
* Are equity-only
* Require relocation outside the United States or India
* Require a PhD unless experience can clearly substitute
* Are primarily frontend-only
* Are primarily data analyst / BI roles
* Are primarily prompt engineering without infrastructure or software depth
* Are MLOps-only with no AI systems, inference, or platform engineering component
* Require active security clearance
* Require 8–10+ years when listed as a hard requirement
* Are internships, apprenticeships, or student-only roles
